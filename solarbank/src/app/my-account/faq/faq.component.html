Frequently asked questions

Q: Do I need Solar to participate?
A: Yes. You need a means to generate energy.
Q: Do I need a battery to participate?
A: Yes. You need a means to store your energy that you want to sell.
Q: Do I have to be an AGL customer?
A: Yes. This is so that you can sell your excess energy to AGL
Q: Why don’t I just sell it to AGL?
A: You can, but you only get the standard tariff (e.g. 6c per kWh in Victoria, or 24c per kWh in SA, etc). By signing up, you could get paid an extra $3,000 per year*.
Q: How is the spot price calculated?
A: The sell price fluctuates based on supply and demand
Q: Why do you need access to my solar battery?
A: This is so that we can automatically export out your energy at the price you nominate.
Q: What is the Current Balance view?
A: You can view how much $ your system has generated since you participated
Q: When will the $ hit my account?
A: You can withdraw your Current Balance at any time. If withdrawn, we will pay your Solar Credits immediately. Think of this as your Personal ATM
Q: Can I be alerted when the sell price is high?
A: Yes. You can request to be have an alert notification sent out
Q: What’s in it for AGL?
A: AGL benefits from a smoothed out usage.
Q: Does it cost anything to participate?
A: No. You are free to elect to sell whenever you choose.
